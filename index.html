Tangled is a 2010 American 3D computer-animated film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Loosely based on the German fairy tale Rapunzel in the collection of folk tales published by the Brothers Grimm, it is the 50th Disney animated feature film.
Directed by Nathan Greno and Byron Howard, produced by Roy Conli, with a screenplay written by Dan Fogelman, staring the voices of Mandy Moore, Zachary Levi and Donna Murphy.

After receiving the healing powers of a magical flower,
baby Princess Rapunzel is kidnapped from the palace in the middle of the night by Mother Gothel

As the years pass, Rapunzel becomes fascinated by an annual event that seems to happen only on her birthday.
That night, every year, she witnesses thousands of floating lights in the sky, brighter than the stars and beautiful in all their colors.

When the kingdom's most-wanted bandit, Flynn Rider, hides in a convenient tower, he immediately becomes a captive of Rapunzel. Together they begin a whirlwind adventure.
