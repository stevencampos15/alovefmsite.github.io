Tangled is a 2010 American 3D computer-animated film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. Loosely based on the German fairy tale Rapunzel in the collection of folk tales published by the Brothers Grimm, it is the 50th Disney animated feature film.
Directed by Nathan Greno and Byron Howard, produced by Roy Conli, with a screenplay written by Dan Fogelman, staring the voices of Mandy Moore, Zachary Levi and Donna Murphy.

After receiving the healing powers of a magical flower,
baby Princess Rapunzel is kidnapped from the palace in the middle of the night by Mother Gothel

As the years pass, Rapunzel becomes fascinated by an annual event that seems to happen only on her birthday.
That night, every year, she witnesses thousands of floating lights in the sky, brighter than the stars and beautiful in all their colors.

When the kingdom's most-wanted bandit, Flynn Rider, hides in a convenient tower, he immediately becomes a captive of Rapunzel. Together they begin a whirlwind adventure.

Rapunzel spends most of her life in a tower with her chameleon friend, Pascal, imagining the world outside. Each year on her birthday, she sees from her tower window, floating lights comming from the castle, she then wonders what they are and what they mean and why they only come on her birthday.

At first, Flynn comes off as cocky, arrogant, and a little self-centered. As an experienced thief, he is skilled in escaping the authorities, hiding out, and stealing right from under people's noses. However, as he begins to fall in love with Rapunzel, he transforms into a more compassionate, chivalrous, and trustworthy person, and changes his priorities.

Pascal is a true and loyal friend to Rapunzel. Even though he can't communicate verbally, he is one of the driving forces in pushing Rapunzel to leave the tower. Pascal is constantly cheering Rapunzel up, rooting her on, and refusing to allow her to take steps backwards.

Maximus is a dedicated guard horse who will stop at nothing to take down the criminal he is after. Maximus is brave, daring, bold and not at all cowardly even being more feared than his captain. Although Maximus can be extremely tough, he has a large heart and even becomes best friends with his former arch enemy Flynn Ryder as they join together to reunite Rapunzel with her family.
